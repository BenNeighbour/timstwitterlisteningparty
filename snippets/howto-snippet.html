    Here's how the listening parties work LIVE

    A few folks asking how these work. They’re really easy:

    Stream or play the album in question and follow me and the relevant twitterer(s) and watch the tweets in real time. Ask questions/ share memories etc using #timstwitterlisteningparty. Nowt complicated. That’s it ; )

    — Tim Burgess (@Tim_Burgess) March 26, 2020
    The schedule below shows which accounts to follow so that you pick up relevant tweets on your twitter timeline. Look out for Tim's tweet telling you when to start the music.
    Follow @Tim_Burgess

    #TimsTwitterListeningParty


    If you want to see thoughts and memories from everyone who is listening along, look for the hashtag #TimsTwitterListeningParty

    Use this hashtag yourself, if you want to tweet along or ask questions.

    Listening Party Twitter List


    If you follow a lot of accounts, so your timeline is busy with tweets not related to the listening party, there is a curated Twitter list that will show just those tweets from Tim and the relevant artists. This makes it easier to see just the relevant tweets while you listen along.

    The list is updated every day with all the artists for all the parties that day and can also be found on the site here
    List managed by @gingerbeardman

    Replay Page


    If you miss, or want to relive, a listening party we have a replay page where you can view the tweets sequenced and played back in real-time. If you set the album playing at the start, the tweets will be in sync as you listen along.

    Browse or search for your favourites, or even ask Tim to choose one for you at random.

    Look out for tweets like this from the @LISTENING_PARTY account giving links to the replays.

    Replay available @oasis : Oasis : The Masterplan at https://t.co/9gkAzRSa54 #TimsTwitterListeningParty

    — Tim's Listening Party (@LlSTENlNG_PARTY) May 6, 2020
    Follow @LlSTENlNG_PARTY
    Replay page developed and maintained by @andrewb1970

    Archive Page


    If you miss a listening party and just want to read through all the tweets, then our archive page and search page has links to the full set of tweets from Tim and the artists for you to read at your own speed.
    List & Archive page developed and maintained by @matbroughty
